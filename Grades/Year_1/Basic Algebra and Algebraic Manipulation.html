Basic Algebra and Algebraic Manipulation

  • Algebraic manipulation refers to the manipulation of algebraic expressions, often into a simpler form or a form which is more easily handled and dealt with. It is one of the most basic, necessary and important skills to use when solving math problems.
  • An algebraic expression is a combination of mathematical symbols and numerical values which are related to each other.
Learning Objectives
  • Use terms like expressions, variables, constant terms, and coefficient appropriately.
  • Use letters to represent algebra in formulae and expressions.
  • Evaluate algebraic expressions, given the value of their variables.
  • Manipulate and simplify simple algebraic expressions including algebraic fractions.
  • Factorise algebraic expressions by "taking out common factor" and "grouping".
