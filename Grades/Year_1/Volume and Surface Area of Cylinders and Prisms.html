Volume and Surface Area

  • A prism is a 3-D object with two equal parallel faces(base). If all the faces excluding the base are rectangular, the prism is also known as a right prism
  • A cylinder is a 3-D object that looks like a prism except that the bases are circles, and that the cylinder has a curved lateral surface instead of parallelogram sides.
Learning Objectives
  • Find the volume and surface area of prisms.cylinders and composite solids
  • Solve problems involving volume, surface area and density of these solids
    • Prisms
  • Volume of prism: `Base Area xx Height`
  • Surface area of prism: `2A + P xx H`
    • Cylinders
  • Volume of cylinder: `pir^2h`
  • Surface area of cylinder: `2A + P xx H`
  • Curved surface area of cylinder: `2pir^2`
